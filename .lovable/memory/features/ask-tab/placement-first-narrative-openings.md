name: Behavior-First, Placement-As-Reason Openings
description: Every narrative_section body must OPEN with lived behavior in sentence 1, then name the chart placement that causes it in sentence 2 — never the other way around
type: preference
---
Every narrative body paragraph must follow this formula:
1. First sentence: lived behavior or recognizable pattern in plain human language. The reader feels seen.
2. Second sentence: name the specific placement that causes it (sign + house + ruler if relevant). The reader learns the astrology.
3. Then continue into broader patterns, shadow dynamics, and lived experience.

Example: "You're not drawn to chaos or drama as a baseline — and that comes from your Capricorn 7th house ruled by Saturn in Cancer in your 1st."

FORBIDDEN: Opening with "Your 7th house is...", "Your Venus in...", "Your relationship blueprint is built on...", or any astrology-first sentence. The reader must feel recognized in sentence 1, then learn the astrology in sentence 2.

This supersedes the older Placement-First rule and applies to ALL topics — relationship, career, health, spiritual, money, relocation, timing, general.

Implemented in `supabase/functions/ask-astrology/index.ts` SYSTEM_PROMPT (BEHAVIOR-FIRST, PLACEMENT-AS-REASON RULE block).
